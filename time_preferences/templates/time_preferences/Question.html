Below, you will find a table with the 10 decisions for you to make. 

The decisions all have a similar format. For example, look at Decision 1 at the top. Option A pays 100 points one month from now and Option B pays 105.09 points thirteen months from now. If you choose Option B you will earn an annual return of 5% on the 100 you choose to receive 13 months from now. Since this is compounded quarterly your annual effective interest rate is 5.09%. The only difference in the other nine decisions is that as you move down the table the payoffs (and annual interest rate) for Option B increase. You have a 1-in-10 chance of being paid for one of the decision problems which will be decided by a random draw.

 

For each of the 10 decisions, please choose either A or B and when finished press "Submit"
